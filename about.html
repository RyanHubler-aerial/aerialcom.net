About Aerial Communications

Combining high-availability enterprise IT expertise with the local support and care required by commercial businesses.

Our Mission

At Aerial Communications, we strive to provide cutting-edge IT solutions for commercial businesses, no matter their size. We make technology easy, manageable, and cost-effective by offering structured, robust technology implementations.

We are dedicated to serving our local Southcentral Pennsylvania community, including local businesses, municipalities, non-profits, and law enforcement agencies. We custom-fit our services to protect those who support our community.

What Separates Us?

We operate with the capability of a major IT integrator combined with the individual responsiveness of a local firm. Our team stays focused on delivering stable, secure systems without the overhead and sluggish support times of corporate conglomerates.

Whether we are setting up IP phone systems, optical uplinks, or configuring network firewalls, we prioritize transparency and technical excellence.
