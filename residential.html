Walk-In Services

Residential IT Support

Walk-in computer diagnostics, cleanups, and data recovery for personal computers and household devices.

Huntingdon Location Exclusive

Please note that all residential and personal device repairs are performed exclusively at our Huntingdon Office. Our Bedford and Altoona offices focus solely on commercial enterprise and field integrations.

Offered Services

PC Cleanups

Remove viruses, adware, tracking cookies, and malicious applications. We optimize system registry files, clean startup items, and configure security tools.

Data Recovery

Retrieve deleted files, photos, and crucial business documents from broken or failing hard disk drives, SSDs, and storage cards.

Device Diagnostics & Setup

Perform hardware evaluations, replace broken laptop screens, install RAM or solid-state drive upgrades, and set up brand new retail computers.

OS Reinstallation

Clean reinstallations of Windows and macOS, driver configuration, system updates, and basic application setup to make older machines run like new.

Service Exclusions: Please note that we do not offer residential telephone line installations, landline configurations, mobile phone or tablet hardware repairs (such as cracked glass screen replacement or charging port fixes), or voice network routing for home users.
